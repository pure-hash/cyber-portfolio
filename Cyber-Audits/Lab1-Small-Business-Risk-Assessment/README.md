# Lab 1 – Small Business Risk Assessment

## Overview
Conduct a basic cybersecurity risk assessment for a small business environment.

## Objectives
- Identify assets and critical systems
- Evaluate potential risks
- Document and prioritize findings

## Tools Used
- Spreadsheets for asset tracking
- Risk assessment frameworks
- Python or PowerShell scripts for data collection

## Steps
1. Identify and inventory assets
2. Analyze potential risks and vulnerabilities
3. Document findings in `/datasets`
4. Take screenshots in `/images` as necessary
5. Summarize risks and recommendations

## Findings
- Assets cataloged
- Risk assessment completed
- Findings documented

## Skills Demonstrated
- Risk identification  
- Documentation  
- Security analysis  

## Folder Structure
- **`/datasets`** – Audit data and reports  
- **`/images`** – Screenshots or visual documentation  
- **`/scripts`** – Automation scripts for assessments
